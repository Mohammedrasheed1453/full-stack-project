About Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham (Amrita University) is a leading private deemed university in India, with a strong multi-campus presence across Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Andhra Pradesh, Haryana, and more.

Established in 2003, it offers over 250 programs with world-class infrastructure, extensive research facilities, and a commitment to social impact inspired by its humanitarian founder, Sri Mata Amritanandamayi Devi (Amma).

The university has earned top ranks and national accreditation, collaborating globally for excellence. Its holistic education integrates technology, culture, and sustainability, producing influential leaders, researchers, and professionals.
